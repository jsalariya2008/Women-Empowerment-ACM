Stories of Strength

Aruna Roy, The Activist

"She believed she could, so she did."

Aruna Roy is an Indian social activist, professor, union organizer and former civil servant. She is the president of the National Federation of Indian Women and founder–member of the Mazdoor Kisan Shakti Sangathan.

Read her journey →

Falguni Nayar, Founder Nykaa

"Dream big but start small"

Former investment banker Falguni Nayar quit her job to start Nykaa - the online retailer of fashion and beauty products. Falguni Sanjay Nayar is one of two self-made female Indian billionaires. Nayar's accomplishments and success have made her an inspiration to other young business people in India and all around the world.

Read her challenge →

Dr. Prema Dhanraj, Founder Agni Raksha

"Resilience is learning to rise again."

She founded the non-profit organization Agni Raksha in 1999, which focuses on the comprehensive rehabilitation of burn survivors, particularly from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The organization provides medical treatment, counseling, physiotherapy, legal aid, and vocational training, having served over 10,000 victims and identified more than 18,000 since its inception.
