Akka Café

Food & Cafe

Akka Café is an initiative under the National Livelihood Mission, managed by women’s self-help groups like Kamadhenu Women's SHG. These cafés offer hygienic, local food at affordable prices, promoting women's economic empowerment in rural areas.

Mann Deshi Mahila Sahakari Bank

Financial Inclusion & Empowerment

Chetna Gala Sinha founded Mann Deshi Mahila Sahakari Bank, India's first rural women's bank, providing financial services and loans to women entrepreneurs. This initiative has supported numerous women-led businesses, fostering economic independence.

The Mushroom Lady

Farming & Cultivation

Bina Devi, known as 'The Mushroom Lady,' started cultivating mushrooms under her bed due to lack of space. Her success led her to inspire over 1,500 women in 105 villages to take up mushroom farming. She was honored with the Nari Shakti Samman for her contribution to agriculture.
